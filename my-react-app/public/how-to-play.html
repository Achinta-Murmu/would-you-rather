How to play

Would You Rather is a hypothetical talking game where players are asked questions that start with "Would you rather" and offer two options. Players can choose questions that are silly, thoughtful, or completely hypothetical. For example, you might ask "Would you rather have the power to fly or the gift of invisibility?" . After the question is asked, everyone else has to choose one option.
